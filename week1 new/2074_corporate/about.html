About Left

In the heart of a bustling metropolitan city, Coporate Bizz emerged as a beacon of artistic expression in 1985. Founded by visionary art enthusiast Isabella Harper, the gallery aimed to bridge the gap between talented artists and art connoisseurs. Isabella's passion for the arts and her keen eye for talent quickly set Corporate Bizz apart. The gallery's early years were marked by a commitment to showcasing diverse artistic styles. Isabella Harper curated a collection that ranged from traditional oil paintings to avant-garde sculptures, creating a space that appealed to a wide audience. This commitment to diversity earned Corporate Bizz a reputation for being a hub of artistic exploration.

Our Right Team

Recognizing the importance of art education and community engagement, Gallery Elegance initiated various programs to support emerging artists and educate the public. Art workshops, artist talks, and partnerships with local schools became integral components of the gallery's mission. The gallery's commitment to nurturing the next generation of artists and art enthusiasts strengthened its ties to the community.


Our History

As Corporate Bizz celebrates its 38th year in 2023, it stands as a testament to the power of artistic passion and dedication. The gallery continues to thrive, representing a diverse array of artists and serving as a cultural hub for the city. The legacy of Isabella Harper lives on through the vibrant and ever-evolving space she envisioned decades ago. Corporate Bizz remains committed to its founding principles – showcasing exceptional art, fostering creativity, and connecting artists with those who appreciate their work. In an age where the art world is constantly evolving, Corporate Bizz stands as a beacon of continuity, reminding us all of the enduring power of artistic expression.

Left Side Team

Diverse Collection: Corporate Bizz prides itself on curating a diverse and eclectic collection of artworks. This includes traditional paintings, avant-garde sculptures, digital art forms, and experimental installations. The curatorial team, led by the vision of Isabella Harper and later by Sophia Harper, carefully selects pieces that represent a broad spectrum of artistic styles, ensuring that the gallery remains a space for exploration and appreciation of different forms of art.
